Welcome

multiStepForm is an angular module to create multi step forms and wizards. Create your steps like your would create your views with ngRoute or ui-router!

It is lightweight (6kb minified) but extremely versatile and powerful, and makes no assumption on how you want to design your multi-step form or wizard. Discover what it is capable of with the following examples and documentation.
