How to invoke finish or cancel

A multi-step form instance is ended by invoking cancel() or finish() on multiStepFormInstance. Those two functions are also available in your scopes: $cancel() and $finish()

By default, if a multi-step form is ended, it will be destroyed and will disappear. There is no possibility to bring it back and reloading the view will only create a new instance of it.

This example has two instances running of a multi-step form (it demonstrates we can run multiple multi-step forms in the same view). The first one (this one) has no callbacks defined and finishing or cancelling with destroy it. The later one has onCancel and onFinish callbacks defined: they will alert you on the outcome, and navigate away.
